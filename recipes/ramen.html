Tsukemen Ramen

Home

Tsukemen consists of cold noodles and a rich broth (dipping sauce). You dip your noodles into the broth and eat them just like you eat Zaru Soba.

Ingredients

  • Pork troters
  • Green onion (green portion only)
  • Smashed garlic
  • Smashed ginger
  • A lot of water
  • Dried anchovies
  • Bonito flake powder

Steps

  1. Cook ramen noodles per the instructions on the pack.
  2. Rinse the noodles under running water to cool down and remove sliminess, then drain well.
  3. Place the noodles on a serving plate with sliced pork belly, boiled eggs, and green onions on the side.
  4. Put Tsukedare Base in a small bowl and add Tonkotsu Broth to it. Scatter sesame seeds in the centre of the broth if using.
  5. Serve immediately.
